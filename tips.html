Stardew Valley 10 Tips for beginners

Tip 1

When you first start the game spend your 500g (Gold) on seeds to gain more money.

Tip 2

Save 1 of everything, it will be needed in the future.

Tip 3

Stick around for the first winter to unlock secret notes. Secret notes hold secrets of each character and useful tips.

Tip 4

Hearts with villagers can be used for heart events. Plus if you get your hearts up you can date some of the villagers.

Tip 5

When you get your first prismatic shard DON'T donate it to the museum. The prismatic shard is used to get the best sword in the game.

Tip 6

Always check the TV. The TV can tell you if today is a lucky day, livin on the land tips for the season, recipes and the weather for the next day.

Tip 7

Pierres and Clint's shop is cheaper in the first year. Take up mining in the first year so you can upgrade your tools that year for a cheaper price.

Tip 8

On your lucky days check the trash for items.

Tip 9

Remember Birthdays they can help build relationships with characters.

Tip 10

Use quality sprinklers to gain a higher chance of gaining massive plants.
