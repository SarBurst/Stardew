Stardew Valley Introduction

Stardew Valley is a 1-2 player game. The basic story is that your grandfather dies and he leaves you a farm in pelician town. The objective of the game is to explore, build up your farm and build relationships with the villagers.

Stardew Valley can be played on the switch, pc and the ps4.
